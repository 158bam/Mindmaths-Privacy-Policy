Privacy Policy

MindMaths - Math Learning App

Last updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to MindMaths ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our MindMaths mobile application.

MindMaths is an educational math quiz application designed to help users improve their mathematical skills through interactive quizzes and exercises.

2. Information We Collect

MindMaths is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information to provide you with the best possible experience:

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Quiz Performance Data: We collect your quiz scores, correct/incorrect answers, and response times to provide personalized learning experiences and track your progress.
  • App Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, including which operations (addition, subtraction, multiplication, division) you practice and difficulty levels you choose.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Basic device information such as device model, operating system version, and app version for technical support and app optimization.
  • App Performance Data: Crash reports and performance metrics to improve app stability and user experience.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Social media accounts
  • Financial information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide Educational Services: Generate appropriate math questions based on your selected difficulty level and operation type.
  • Track Progress: Monitor your learning progress and provide feedback on your performance.
  • Improve App Functionality: Analyze usage patterns to enhance the app's features and user experience.
  • Technical Support: Diagnose and resolve technical issues to ensure smooth app operation.
  • App Optimization: Improve app performance and stability based on usage data.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data security is our priority:

  • Local Storage: Most of your quiz data is stored locally on your device to ensure privacy and offline functionality.
  • Secure Transmission: Any data transmitted to our servers (if applicable) is encrypted using industry-standard protocols.
  • Data Protection: We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction of your data.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Quiz Performance Data: Stored locally on your device and retained until you uninstall the app or clear app data.
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained for app improvement purposes.
  • Crash Reports: Retained for a limited time to resolve technical issues.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access Your Data: You can view your quiz history and performance data within the app.
  • Delete Your Data: You can clear all app data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data through your device settings.
  • Opt-Out: You can disable analytics collection through your device settings (if available).
  • Control Permissions: You can manage app permissions through your device settings.

7. Children's Privacy

MindMaths is designed to be safe for users of all ages, including children:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.
  • The app does not require personal information to function.
  • All content is educational and age-appropriate.
  • Parents can control and monitor their children's app usage through device settings.

8. Third-Party Services

MindMaths may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services: For app distribution and basic analytics (if applicable).
  • Crash Reporting: To identify and fix technical issues.

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy

Your continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

10. International Users

MindMaths is operated from and may be accessed from various locations around the world. By using our app, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries other than your own, where privacy laws may be different.
